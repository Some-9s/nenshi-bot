Dollars and Sense

We have a desire to optimize for OpEx over CapEx. This service would be highly elastic and should make use of as much OSS as possible. Ideally we want to come in under the $75k limit to avoid the bid process (we are lazy).

Item Value Cost
Google Big Query Fast Web Based Queries of ODS Free for less than 1TB of data storage and Free for less than 1TB of Queries
AWS EC2 hosting the parsing and reply engine ~$20/month
