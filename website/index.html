How Does It Do This?

Nenshi-bot is built on a simple stack.

  • Twitter

    Social Mediums!

    Tweets directed at @Nenshi-bot kick off the process - answers are given in a reply.

  • Lita.io

    A Ruby Chatbot

    Requests are heard by a lita chatbot and directed to the appropriate handler.

  • Data

    The City of Calgary's Open Data Catalgoue

    Required information is retrieved from the City's Open Data Catalogue

  • Backend Magic

    Explaining the smoke and mirrors.

    Select datasets from the ODC are loaded in Google BigQuery and queried from there.
